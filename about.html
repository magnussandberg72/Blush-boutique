About Sandra

The heart behind Blush Boutique 💗

Sandra is a friendly, passionate woman originally from Germany, now running her fashion boutique in Florida, USA. Her store “Blush Boutique” is located in Aventura Mall, where she curates elegant clothing, handbags, and accessories for women who love timeless style.

The boutique reflects Sandra’s personality — warm, genuine, and full of charm. Every product she selects is chosen with love, aiming to make her customers feel confident and beautiful.

Sandra works hard to balance both her online store and her physical boutique. She also appreciates the support from kind people around the world who value her dedication and vision.

You can reach her through email, PayPal, or even send a friendly message if you’d like to collaborate or support her brand.

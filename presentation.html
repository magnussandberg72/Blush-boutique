💖 Who is Fatimah?

Fatimah is a young woman in Ghana living under difficult conditions. This page tells her story and how you can help.

Help with food money for Fatimah 🙏🏼

Fatimah is a kind, loving woman, trying to do her best.

She has worked in a supermarket, and most recently at a stone/gravel quarry, with a monthly salary of about 70 USD.

She covers the monthly bills and medicine for her sick mother, but the work money just isn’t enough to cover both monthly bills, And basic food costs. please send $10, to oatmeal and milk 🙏🏼.

My name is Magnus, I am from Sweden, and I got to know Fatimah in 2024. She is my friend, and I have helped her with food money now and then. As I have understood, salaries in Ghana are very low.

All support goes via her mother Mavis Boakye (Fatimah’s mom), directly to Fatimah through MTN Mobile Money and WorldRemit.

Please send some food money to Fatimah through the WorldRemit app. Every gift is a blessing for her and her mom – they are very poor. Thank you for your kindness 🙏🏼

/ Magnus, Fatimah and her mom
